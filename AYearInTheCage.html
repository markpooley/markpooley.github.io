Nic Cage is perhaps America's classiest actor who makes frivolous purchases (Nic Cage Outbids Leo on TRex Skull) has quite a lengthy fimly catalog. The challenge is to watch all of Nic Cage's films in a year, hence the term "A Year in the Cage!". A total of 7,724 mins of Nic Cage cinema is to be watched (that's 129 hours of cinema - 2 hrs longer than Aaron Ralston has his arm trapped in a boulder). Said films have had budgets totalling $2,698,853,762 (estimated), and gross revenue of $3,775,453,284 (estimated).

After each viewing reactions, thoughts, notable quotes, and "Cagisms" (Great lines from Mr. Cage) will be recorded. The "year" will start June 1st, 2015. The chosen start date will hopefully give me adequate time to get our basement entertainment room back together before embarking on what is sure to be a truly epic year of watching all 74 Nic Cage Movies (That's an average of one movie every 5 days)!

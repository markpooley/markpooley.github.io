Dental Service Areas are built from ZCTAs (Zip Code Tabultion Areas). Dental Visits occuring inside and outside each ZCTA are tabulated and a crosswalk is used to determine how ZCTAS are aggregated into representative geographic service areas using both health data and geographic constraints. For a more in-depth explanation of how service areas are created click here.

Localization of Care (LOC) is a ratio of the visits occuring within a service area to all visits observed from members liviving within the service area (some visits occur out of a servic area). Mouse/hover over a service area to find out the LOC and get an idea of the number of ZCTAS making up a particulare service area.

Bicycle Projects

I like bikes. They provide me with a source of transportation and they're cheaper than therapy. I've built one frame from the ground up, and several bikes done several "frame up" builds.


  • Shimergo Conversion

    Doing a Shimgero (Campagonolo 10 speed shifters with Shimano 8 speed drivetrain) on 1974 Schwinn Voyageur II. It's a bike I hardly every ride, but wanted to make it even more flashy.

  • Pugsley Rebuild

    The fatbike that started the movement. This machine is well buitl and thought out, but suffered from a poor paint job and much abuse from the rider. Read about breathing new life in to an old rusted out Surly Pugsley.

  • Handbuilt Project

    Read about building a bike from nothing. Everything from the inspiration and design process, to welding, building wheels, assembling, and taking it for a test ride.

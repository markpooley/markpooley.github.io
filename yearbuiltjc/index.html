*Data may contain inaccuracies due to source availability

The data aren't perfect. Instead of painstakingly checking everything I released the map to so the public could take a look and notify me of any errors they might find

Causes/sources of errors can be attributed to the following:
  • Incorrect data from government resources (notice Kinnick is missing! I'm working on that)
  • For old houses/structures have "estimated" constructions years based on criteria the Assessors use (see comment here)
  • Missing data points. School construction years aren't in parcel or assessor data, and aren't availble on one nice web page like UI data.
  • Spatial join/proximity issues. I geocoded some data scraped from web pages. Buildings with no data were assigned a construction year based on the nearest point, or the median build year of thsoe surrounding it.

If you see any errors drop me a line and I'll work to correct them!
